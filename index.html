Re-Eis

We are an independent team of developers creating accessible interfaces for Android devices.

We develop a range of applications designed to improve the lives of people with various health conditions, as well as support professionals working with them. Our solutions aim to make mobile device usage more accessible, convenient, and safe by enabling alternative input methods such as head movement, eye tracking, and sound-based commands.

Our mission is to help people with motor impairments use smartphones effectively and without barriers.
